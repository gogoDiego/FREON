THE PIRACY PROBLEM

Methods of FingerPrinting

How it works

The Reason why its so hard to crack on pirates is because "how do you prove that someone pirated content?", especially someone who is anonymous? Well in a traditional crime scene you have fingerprints.

These fingerprints leave a unique a trace, sort of like an ID and the way to prove guilt is by connecting these unique finger prints to a suspect. What if there was a way to digitally add the fingerprint for every consumer of content. Well with FREON we are able to identify and prove Illegal redistribution of content through Steganography or “fingerprinting”

Imprint

A imprinted ID tag that modified images/video that conceals a message (the Consumer ID) without being visible to the naked eyes

Metadata

A Metadata consumer ID tag that is inject into the file that holds consumers ID

Visual

Watermark text ID on content

Audio

Inaudible audio that emits a unique consumer ID
